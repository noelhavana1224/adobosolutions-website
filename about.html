How We Started

We started as a VA agency. We became a technology company.

Adobo Solutions was born from a simple frustration: businesses were wasting massive amounts of money on US and UK staff for tasks that talented, English-proficient Filipinos could handle just as well — often better — at a fraction of the cost.

We started matching Filipino VAs with global clients. But we quickly realized the biggest problem wasn't finding talent — it was equipping them with the right tools. Most VA agencies just hire people. We build the systems around them.

So we built AdoBoost — our AI-powered cold email platform — for our own Lead Gen VAs. It worked so well we opened it up to the world. Today it's used by businesses globally as a standalone tool, and it's the secret weapon behind every Adobo Solutions Lead Gen client.

How AdoBoost Was Born

We needed a better tool.
So we built one.

When we started scaling our Lead Gen VA service, we tried every cold email tool on the market. They were either too expensive, too complex, or lacked the AI features our VAs needed to do the job well.

So we built AdoBoost from scratch — specifically designed for the way our VAs work. AI sequence generation, smart warmup, humanized sending, and reply tracking in one place. Our VAs used it internally, clients started asking about it, and we opened it up.
